Who I Am

I am a Computer Science graduate from ZCAS University, Zambia with a genuine obsession with cybersecurity — not as a subject to study, but as something to build, break, and understand from the inside out.

My final year project, NAegisQ, was a fully working quantum-resistant banking security framework. Real post-quantum algorithms (Kyber and Dilithium), a Go backend, PostgreSQL, an Ethereum smart contract anchoring transactions to the blockchain — deployed and tested on enterprise hardware I own. Not a simulation.

Outside of that, I run a full SOC home lab on a Dell R720 server — seven virtual machines, pfSense firewall with proper network segmentation, Active Directory, Wazuh SIEM collecting over 1,800 alerts a day from live endpoint agents, Splunk Enterprise as secondary SIEM, and a dedicated DMZ for purple team attack exercises.

I am currently working as an IT Specialist Intern at Oram Media Dynamics in Lusaka while actively looking for a SOC Analyst or Junior Security Analyst role where I can bring this depth of hands-on experience into a professional team.
